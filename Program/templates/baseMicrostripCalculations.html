Conformal Mapping for Microstrip transmission lines

There is two methods which have to be taking into consideration when calculating the characteristic impedance and effective relative permittivity of a multi-dielectric layered Microstrip transmission line. The condition which dictates which method will be used is if \(\frac{w}{h} > 1\) or is \(\frac{w}{h} < 1\).

$$q_N=1-\sum_{j=M+1}^N-1 q_j-\sum_{i=1}^M q_i$$

$$H_i=\frac{h_i}{h}$$ $$H_j=\frac{h_j}{h}$$

$$V_j=\frac{v_j}{h}$$
